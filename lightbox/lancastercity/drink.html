If the weather's wonderful, head up to Tellus360's roof to relax. Tellus also has live music and a game room on the second floor stuffed with darts, board games, pool, and more

Wacker Brewing Co.
"The Wacker Brewing Company traces it's roots back to 1853 and is documented as the oldest institution of its kind in Lancaster County. After being closed for more than 55 years Wacker Brewing is proud to rekindle Joseph Wacker's legacy by once again offering local, hand crafted quality beer in Lancaster."
417 West Grant Street
(717).617.2711

Springhouse Taproom
Crazy brews at one of Lancaster's first craft beer scenes. Bar snacks are available if you're hungry, but locals go for the brews. Not sure what you want? Grab the huge sampler tray for one of each beer.
25 West King Street
(717).399.4009

Annie Bailey's
The Pub is modeled in the Victorian Dublin style and Lancaster's only authentic Irish Pub. Head to the back of the pub to check out the outdoor deck seating, or upstairs to the second floor if the ground floor barn is crowded.
30 East King Street
(717).393.4000
